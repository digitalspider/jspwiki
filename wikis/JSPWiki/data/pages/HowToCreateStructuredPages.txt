You can get a list of pages that link to a page, using the following plugin:
{{{
[{ReferringPagesPlugin max=1000}]
}}}
See: [HowToPage]\\
See: [https://jspwiki-wiki.apache.org/Wiki.jsp?page=JSPWikiCorePlugins]

Then you add links on the bottom of any page, such as:
{{{
----
[Documentation] | [HowToPage]
}}}
----
